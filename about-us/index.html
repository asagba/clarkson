Vision and Values

Established in 1962 in Lagos Nigeria, we are one of the most experienced insurance advisory service providers in Nigeria. We have continued to evolve in providing value driven and bespoke services to our numerous clients.

In our over sixty years of existence, we have built a strong track record for professionalism and our brand has remained a worthy ambassador of the insurance profession. We have maintained strong professional ethics, sound expertise and technical depth as we provide the best informed professional advice across the whole spectrum of corporate and individual risks.
